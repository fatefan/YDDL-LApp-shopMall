- __mothod :__ "GET"
- __params:__
	- "shopId" --- 商家ID --- 必填 --- int

		- 1001 失败

			- productId --- 产品Id
			- introduce --- 商品介绍
			- price --- 商品价格
			- productName --- 商品名词
		- classification --- 分类 Array 项目是Object
			- name
			- data --- 分类商品数据
